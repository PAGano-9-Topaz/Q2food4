Pinakbet

Step 1: fry porl belly - crispy

Ingredients needed: pork and canola oil

Step 2: Sauté garlic, ginger, tomatoes, and onions

Ingredients needed: Garlic, onions, and leftover oil

- mix shrimp paste with sautéed ingredients

Step 3: add squash, water, and knorr shrimp cube - then cover

ingredients: squash, water, and knorr cube

Step 4: add the vegetables - mix and cover

ingredients: sitaw, ampalaya, eggplants, and okra

Serve

Ingredients

200g porb belly (small pieces)‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎

2 tbsp canola oil

1 small pc red onion (chopped)

1 tbsp ginger (stripped)

3 cloves garlic (chopped)

3 ripe tomatoes (cubed)

½ cup water

1 Knorr Shrimp cube

1 bunch sitaw (cut into 2 inch)

1 small ampalaya (thin slices)

1 eggplant (sliced)

7 okra (sliced)
